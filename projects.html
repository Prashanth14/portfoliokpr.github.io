Green Farm

Green Farm is an online web application for farmers

• Designed and developed a web application for the farmers, which helps them sell crops and make better profits, as mediators are reduced, and benefits the customers who get fresh produce at better prices.

• This application has a farmer page where he can upload the different crops he wants to sell. The customers can view all the crops uploaded by different farmers. They can select the crop and quantity and integrate it into the cart if he is okay with the price. The payment method can be done using Card and PayPal.


Outcomes

• This application, helped to reduce the monopoly buyers of agricultural produce and benefit farmers or consumers.
